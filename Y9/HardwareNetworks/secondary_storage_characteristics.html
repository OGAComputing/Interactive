Scenario A
A university student moves between home, the library, and their university building every day. They need to carry their coursework documents (around 500MB total) between locations and access them on different computers.
Scenario B
A large company needs to back up 80TB of data. The backups only need to be accessed very rarely — perhaps once a year in an emergency. Cost per GB is the most important factor.
Scenario C
A graphic designer needs a storage device built into their laptop. They work with very large video files and need the laptop to boot quickly and open files instantly. Budget is less of a concern.
Scenario D
A wedding videographer wants to give each client a physical copy of their wedding video (around 4GB). The client doesn't need to edit it — just watch it on their TV or laptop. Cost per unit should be kept low.
